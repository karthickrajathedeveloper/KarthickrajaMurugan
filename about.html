Hi, I am
Karthickraja M

I am an Embedded Software Developer with hands-on experience in IoT, microcontrollers, and real-time systems. I hold an MSc in Computer Science with Specialization in Data Analytics from Madurai Kamaraj University and am deeply passionate about building innovative IoT solutions for automation, monitoring, and smart systems.

My technical expertise spans across controllers like ESP32, Raspberry Pi, Arduino UNO/MEGA, and STC15, along with strong knowledge of Python, Embedded C/C++, and Micro-Python. I have extensive experience working with RF modules (WiFi, BLE, LoRa, Zigbee) and network communication protocols like MQTT, HTTP/TCP, and WebSockets to build end-to-end connected ecosystems.

Furthermore, I am an active open-source contributor. I have developed and published the Spell_IoT Library for ESP32/ESP8266 on the Arduino Library Manager, which helps students easily send hardware data to the cloud. I have also authored a Python library on PyPI for interfacing RFID with Raspberry Pi 5. I am a disciplined, self-motivated engineer who enjoys solving complex hardware-software integration problems.
